Hope for Children Redesign Project

UI researcher/UI designer

Project Overview

Hope for Children is a certified charity, serving residents throughout all fifty states mainly focusing on ending child abuse and abuse to others. Our goal is to redesign the Hope for Children website to convey their valuable mission and activities in a more clear, concise, and enticing manner in order to motivate individuals to donate, volunteer, or spread the word about Hope for Children initiatives. Our team gathered qualitative data from 5 or more user interviews and website testing. Quantitative data was gathered through a survey sent out via email and social media over a period of 2 days.

Research

Our team will research and redesign the navigation, Layout, UI to help better convey their purpose and Raise awareness to protect children from domestic Violence and sexual assault. Test Plan We asked the user to complete tasks that encompassed the user flow steps and demonstrate the following: 1. Learn more about the Hope for Children organization and what they do 2. Determine what the organization specifically does to help fight child abuse 3. Sign up to Volunteer 4. Donate to a special cause 5.Find examples of how Hope for Children has helped specific individuals or families
